PUTTING STUDENT-ATHLETES FIRST

During a student-athlete's time in college, there is a constant need to balance academics, athletics, and social activities. Upswing Athletics is here to help ensure that student-athletes are successful in maintaining this balance by providing them with comprehensive online support made available anywhere, anytime. We help them to achieve this with integrity, as we are compliant with NCAA and school-specific academic regulations.

We've worked with numerous colleges to provide real-time academic support to students of all types and skill sets. You are able to expand your own learning center to connect your own academic support faculty with student-athletes online. In addition, we have a network of academic coaches who can provide online academic support in subjects or times when your faculty cannot. All the while, you are able to monitor student-athlete usage, trends, and performance.

A LEARNING STYLE FOR THEIR LIFESTYLE

Gone are the days of completing academic assignments with pen and paper. It's time that academic support starts supporting the technology and mobile lifestyle of student-athletes.



QUICK & EASY TO USE



CUSTOMIZED PORTAL

Student-athletes are greeted with a customized dashboard for finding and scheduling academic support sessions. With single sign-on integration, the student-athlete can use their school credentials to connect.

START SEARCHING

The dynamic search bar allows student-athletes to search for academic support by subject, course name, or name of academic coach. Available options appear as the student-athlete searches, making it easy to see everything available.

Browse & Schedule

After completing a search, student-athletes are able to select the coach that best meets their needs for a session. They can schedule a session for a later date or request a session immediately, if the academic coach is available.

Dynamic Session

Student-athletes are connected in real-time with an academic coach that is able to help them through tutoring, mentoring, and advising. Throughout the process, we ensure full NCAA compliance as well as integration of school-specific regulations.

View Statistics

As an administrator, you are able to view real-time statistics on how often student-athletes are seeking academic support and in what subjects. If your learning center tutors are being utilized, you are able to take into consideration usage trends and potential bottlenecks.

Student Analytics

As sessions are completed, the academic coaches input key academic information about student-athlete understanding and room for improvement within the subject. As an administrator, you have visibility into what aspects of classes are causing the most problems, while gauging their overall performance.



ATHLETICS + ACADEMICS = WIN

By ensuring full compliance with NCAA and school regulations, we can help ease your peace of mind when student-athletes receive academic support.



INCREASE THE CHANCES OF ACADEMIC SUCCESS


We've found that it takes more than just technology to ensure that student-athletes are getting the best possible academic experience that your school can offer. That's why we partner directly with every college to create an engagement and retention plan for student-athletes.

Starting off, it is important to work jointly to build awareness and comfort with the Upswing virtual learning center. That's why we will do in-person training with any of your staff who will be connectiong with student-athletes through Upswing Athletics. Also, we provide a range of training and information materials to students to learn quickly how get connected and start learning.

As sessions are ongoing and completed, we collect information about when and how each student-athlete learns best. This is important for being able to predict when certain student-athletes may need additional academic support and how to best administer that support. Administrators are able to see in real-time various trands and actionable insights, in order to better engage student athletes with academics.
